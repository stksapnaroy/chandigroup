Founder Story

One journey. One work ethic. A growing enterprise.

Nachhattar Singh Chandi's story began in Uttar Pradesh, India, and found its next chapter in California. What started as a courageous move toward opportunity became a disciplined pursuit of ownership, operations, and long-term value creation.

Arriving in a new country required resilience, humility, and relentless focus. Those early years shaped a leadership philosophy rooted in trust, hard work, family values, and the belief that business should create opportunity for people and communities.

1991

A New Beginning

From a small village in Uttar Pradesh to building a successful enterprise in the United States, Nachhattar Singh Chandi's journey is a testament to hard work, resilience, and an unwavering commitment to growth and opportunity.

1994

Entrepreneurial Leap

By acquiring and transforming the gas station where he first worked into an ARCO ampm franchise, Nachhattar Singh Chandi turned opportunity into ownership—laying the foundation for Chandi Group USA.

Growth

Expanding Operations

Driven by operational excellence and customer trust, Chandi Group expanded across California, establishing multiple fuel and convenience locations and creating a strong platform for long-term growth.

Scale

Franchise Growth

Strategic partnerships with leading brands enabled Chandi Group to expand into franchise operations, demonstrating its ability to scale responsibly while delivering exceptional customer experiences.

Assets

Real Estate Development

Chandi Group expanded into real estate development, building a diverse portfolio of commercial, retail, hospitality, and multi-family projects designed to create long-term value and community impact.

Build

Integrated Capabilities

By bringing construction, architecture, engineering, and land surveying under one platform, Chandi Group created an integrated development model focused on efficiency, quality, and long-term value.

Impact

Leadership Beyond Business

Guided by a commitment to service and leadership, Chandi Group continues to support communities through philanthropy, mentorship, and initiatives that create lasting positive impact.

Future

Looking Ahead

Guided by the values that shaped our journey, Chandi Group continues to look ahead with purpose—creating opportunities, fostering innovation, and building projects that contribute to stronger communities and a brighter future.
