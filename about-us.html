Founder Journey

Built from resolve, shaped by opportunity.

Born and raised in a small village in Uttar Pradesh, India, Nachhattar Singh Chandi arrived in the United States in 1991 with a vision of creating a better future through determination and hard work.

His journey reflects the courage of an immigrant entrepreneur and the discipline of a builder. From early operations in California to a diversified enterprise platform, the Chandi Group USA story is anchored in family values, trust, execution, and long-term community contribution.

Nachhattar Singh Chandi Founder, Chandi Group USA
