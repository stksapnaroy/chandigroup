Chandi Group Business Verticals

A diversified portfolio driving growth.

At Chandi Group USA, our approach to business has always been guided by a simple belief: lasting success is built by creating value for the people and communities we serve. Over the years, what started as a single entrepreneurial venture has grown into a diversified organization with interests spanning real estate development, franchise operations, fuel services, hospitality, construction, and professional services.

Each business within our portfolio reflects the same principles that have shaped our journey from the beginning—integrity, hard work, innovation, and a commitment to excellence. While our industries may differ, our purpose remains the same: to build businesses that generate opportunity, foster growth, and contribute positively to the communities throughout California where we live and work.

03
Fuel & Convenience Operations

The business foundation built through reliability, speed, and service.

Fuel and convenience operations represent the foundation upon which Chandi Group USA was built. What began with a single gas station has expanded into a network of businesses dedicated to providing dependable service and convenience to customers across California.

We understand that these locations play an important role in everyday life. Whether someone is fueling up for their daily commute, purchasing essential items, or stopping in during a busy day, our goal is to create welcoming environments defined by efficiency, reliability, and exceptional customer care.

By continuously investing in our operations and prioritizing the customer experience, we remain focused on meeting the changing needs of the communities we serve.

04
Car Wash Operations

Express tunnel facilities engineered for convenience.

As consumer expectations continue to evolve, Chandi Group USA has expanded into express tunnel car wash operations designed to offer convenience without compromising quality.

Our facilities are built to provide efficient, dependable service that helps customers maintain their vehicles while fitting seamlessly into their daily routines. By integrating modern technology, streamlined processes, and a customer-focused approach, we aim to deliver a car wash experience that is both practical and enjoyable.

This expansion reflects our ongoing commitment to identifying opportunities that enhance convenience and create additional value for our customers.

05
Quick Service Restaurants

Hospitality translated into everyday scale.

Food has a unique ability to bring people together, and our quick service restaurant operations are centered around creating memorable experiences built on quality, consistency, and hospitality.

Through partnerships with established restaurant brands, we operate locations that serve diverse communities while maintaining a strong emphasis on operational excellence and guest satisfaction. From the moment customers walk through our doors, our focus is on delivering friendly service, quality products, and welcoming environments that encourage repeat visits.

By investing in our teams and continuously improving our operations, we strive to exceed expectations while contributing to the growth of the communities in which we operate

Construction

Execution capability that turns development vision into built assets.

Our construction capabilities play an essential role in bringing our development vision to life. By maintaining in-house expertise, we are able to oversee projects with greater efficiency, accountability, and attention to detail.

From pre-construction planning to final execution, our teams work collaboratively to ensure projects are delivered safely, responsibly, and to the highest standards of quality. This integrated approach enables us to navigate complex developments while maintaining consistency across every phase of the process.

For us, construction is about more than building structures—it's about creating spaces that serve people, support businesses, and stand the test of time.
